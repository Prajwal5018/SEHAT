# SEHAT

## Smart Healthcare Workforce Intelligence Platform

SEHAT is an AI-powered healthcare platform designed to help public hospitals efficiently manage workforce, predict staffing bottlenecks, and optimize resource allocation before patient care is affected.

---

## Problem Statement

Public hospitals often face:

- Staff burnout due to long working hours
- Unexpected staffing shortages
- Uneven resource allocation
- Sudden patient surges
- Delayed operational decisions

Existing hospital systems mainly store data but do not predict future workforce challenges or recommend proactive solutions.

---

## Our Solution

SEHAT transforms hospital management from reactive decision-making to proactive workforce intelligence.

The platform continuously monitors hospital operations, predicts staffing challenges, and provides AI-powered recommendations to optimize workforce and resource allocation.

---

## Key Features

- Real-time Workforce Monitoring
- Burnout Risk Prediction
- Staffing Bottleneck Prediction
- Hospital Digital Twin Simulation
- AI-powered Resource Allocation
- Intelligent Decision Support
- Biometric Attendance Integration
- Department-wise Workforce Analytics

---

## How It Works

1. Staff attendance captured through Biometric Authentication
2. Hospital data collected from HMS
3. AI analyzes workload and workforce utilization
4. Burnout and staffing shortages are predicted
5. Digital Twin simulates emergency scenarios
6. AI generates staffing and resource recommendations
7. Administrators approve and implement suggested actions
